Program Introduction


Introduction to Program. This Building Construction Specialist Certificate Program is designed to assess your mastery of the competencies and skills necessary for employment in construction offices or construction job sites. By the end of this program, you will have acquired the ability to read and interpret drawings and complete construction estimates needed for pursuing a career in building inspection and quality control.
Competencies. Upon the completion of this program you will be able to master competencies in the following areas:

  • Construction Drawings, Analysis and Interpretation
  • Construction Methods, Materials-Analysis and Interpretation
  • Cost Estimating – Checking, Examining and Recording
  • Communication Skills – Understanding and Knowledge of Construction Reports and Project Coordination
